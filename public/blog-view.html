Del Zotto Acquires Gulf
Coast Ready Mix Assets

Homosassa, FL, January 3, 2025

The Del Zotto Group, LLC proudly announces the acquisition of Gulf Coast Ready Mix, LLC, located at 8778 S Jump Ct, Homosassa, FL. This strategic acquisition marks the group's continued expansion and success in the Central Florida concrete industry.

The purchase, finalized on January 3, 2025, was structured as an asset acquisition; all employees of Gulf Coast Ready Mix are intended to be retained. The facility now operates under the name Del Zotto Concrete.

"As a citrus county resident myself, we are excited to be a part of the Citrus County business community and to contribute to serving my neighbors."

— Elizabeth Del Zotto

Founded in 2003, Gulf Coast Ready Mix, LLC has been a trusted supplier of ready-mix concrete and concrete blocks, serving Citrus County and surrounding areas, including Levy, Hernando, Marion, and Sumter counties. Known for its strong reputation in safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, the company provides tailored concrete mix designs to meet diverse project needs.

"We are thrilled to welcome all of the employees of Gulf Coast Ready Mix and Block to the Del Zotto family. This milestone wouldn't have been possible without the hard work and dedication of the owners and staff from both Del Zotto and Gulf Coast, and I want to personally thank everyone involved in making this acquisition a success. We're excited to collaborate with our new team members as we expand the Del Zotto family of companies to Florida's Nature Coast and beyond,"

— Philip Del Zotto

The Del Zotto Family has built a reputation for excellence in the Precast concrete and form industry.

The family has a 50-year legacy in producing precast stormwater, sanitary structures and form manufacturing. In more recent years, the family has expanded its operations to include the manufacturing and delivery of block and ready-mix concrete in Central Florida. This strategic shift reflects their commitment to meeting the growing demands of the regional construction market while positively impacting their customers with their extensive industry experience.

The move to expand their focus on block and ready-mix delivery has enabled the family to deepen their local presence and better serve contractors across the area.

At the heart of this expansion are the six Del Zotto siblings...

At the heart of this expansion are the six Del Zotto siblings—Philip, Mary, Mario, Abigail, Elizabeth, and Miela.

At the heart of this expansion are the six Del Zotto siblings—Philip, Mary, Mario, Abigail, Elizabeth, and Miela—who equally share operational responsibilities across their ventures. Their unique partnership and dedication to preserving their family's legacy have allowed them to adapt and grow, continuing to honor their long-standing values while exploring new opportunities in the industry. A foundation of expertise and reliability has been key to their success.

This acquisition is only the beginning of the Del Zotto family's plans for continued growth in the concrete industry. The acquisition of Gulf Coast Ready Mix is the fourth concrete-focused business in their portfolio in the United States, showcasing the strength of their family bond, shared vision, and unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation in the concrete industry.

The Del Zotto Concrete businesses include:

  • Del Zotto Blocks, LLC

    Del Zotto Blocks, LLC specializes in concrete block manufacturing and delivery, providing high-quality products and related materials to local and regional markets. With a strong focus on reliability and service, Del Zotto Block supports construction and building supply needs across its service area.

  • Del Zotto Products, LLC

    As a nationwide leader in concrete form manufacturing and concrete pipe production, Del Zotto Products, LLC delivers innovative solutions for infrastructure and construction projects across the U.S. Known for its exceptional quality and expertise, Del Zotto Products is a trusted partner for contractors and developers nationwide.

  • Del Zotto Concrete, LLC

    Del Zotto Concrete, LLC operates a massive fleet of mixer trucks to deliver ready-mix concrete to contractors throughout Central Florida and surrounding areas. Committed to efficiency and excellence, the company ensures timely delivery of premium concrete to meet the demands of diverse construction projects.
