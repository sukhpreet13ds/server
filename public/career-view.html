Production Laborer

Ocala Full-time

Role Description

Del Zotto Concrete is seeking a hardworking and reliable Production Laborer to support our concrete production team. This role helps with the production, finishing, handling, and preparation of concrete products while maintaining a clean, safe, and efficient work environment.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist with the production and finishing of concrete products
  • Prepare forms, molds, and work areas for concrete pouring
  • Pour, spread, level, and smooth concrete as needed
  • Remove excess concrete and clean finished surfaces
  • Assist with installing production elements such as steel plates, anchor bolts, reinforcements, and related components
  • Apply sealants, hardening agents, or other finishing materials when required
  • Remove products from forms or molds once cured
  • Clean, maintain, and organize tools, equipment, and production areas
  • Operate basic hand tools, power tools, and production equipment safely
  • Assist with loading, unloading, moving, and staging materials
  • Follow all safety procedures and company production standards
  • Report equipment issues, material concerns, or safety hazards to supervisors
  • Work as part of a team to meet production goals and deadlines

Qualifications:

  • Previous concrete, construction, manufacturing, or general labor experience preferred
  • Ability to lift, carry, bend, stand, and work in a physically active environment
  • Comfortable working outdoors or in a production setting
  • Ability to follow instructions and safety procedures
  • Dependable, punctual, and willing to learn
  • Basic knowledge of hand tools and construction materials is a plus

Why Join WaveCrest Masonry Services?

  • Work on high-end, detail-driven masonry projects
  • Be part of a team that values craftsmanship, quality, and client satisfaction
  • Competitive salary and benefits
  • Opportunities for growth and continued professional development
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment

Ideal Candidate

The ideal candidate is hands-on, dependable, and willing to work hard as part of a team. This person takes pride in quality work and understands the importance of safety, consistency, and attention to detail.
